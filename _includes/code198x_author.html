About the author

I'm Steve Hill — a developer, writer, and lifelong lover of vintage computers. The first machine I owned was a ZX Spectrum, and it lit the spark that made me want to build software. I’ve worked professionally with modern stacks like Ruby on Rails, but I’ve always carried a torch for the classics.

Code Like It’s 198X is my way of finally doing what I always wanted to do: make games — and learn everything from the foundations up, one system at a time.

You can follow my journey here or at stevehill.xyz.
